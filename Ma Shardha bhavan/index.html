DESCRIPTION

"MA SARADA BHAVAN" Is a male P.G(paying guest) cum students hostel in Maulali,Sealdah area of Central Kolkata. The "Bhavan" is mainly built keeping in mind the needs of the young school and college students but the "Bhavan" also do greet its arms for young male professionals and budding aspirants who are keen to launch their careers.Being a male students hostel at the heart of Kolkata the "Bhavan" provides all the daily amenities that a student requires. On top of that the peaceful and hazle free environment of the "Bhavan" enhances the student's urge to study. As majority of students come for higher studies, industrial training program and jobs from other part of the world, so we have made necessary arrangements for their stay, a home away from home at our PG.To know about our services click here



WHY CHOOSE US?

Dear Parents,

We can very well understand your pain and worries when your "loved ones" are detached from you for their studies or job in other cities. First and foremost you are concerned about their safety and then food and lodging etc. The accommodation for your children is nowhere else but in our own home and not a rented one. This is 3 stored building and it is entirely owned by us.You feel like your second home in the city of Kolkata at taltala. We have designed the lay out, keeping in mind the comfortable stay of your children's needs and their requirements with all necessary infrastructure. We never believe in saying "No" to our guests, and try to provide them in best possible manner. Last but not least, we will deliver best to your children.
